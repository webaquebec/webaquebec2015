Kevin Nilson

The New Web

Jeu 19 15h 10 Salle Bell · Keynotes et ateliers

Have you ever wondered what’s new in the web?  Have you ever wondered what the future of the web will look like?  The web today is very different that it was just a few years ago.  The web continues to evolve and change faster than ever and now covers browsers, mobile, and televisions.  There are several simple techniques, methodologies and frameworks that can be used to make your website perform well in the new web.  In this talk Kevin Nilson will talk about current and future trends and cover some of the best practices to build your web application for the new web.

À propos
du Conférencier

Kevin Nilson

Kevin works at Google as the lead of the Chromecast Technical Solutions Engineer team.  Prior to joining Google Kevin was the VP of Engineering at just.me, a mobile social startup funded by Google Ventures.  Kevin is a Java Champion and three time JavaOne Rock Star.  Kevin has spoken at conferences such as JavaOne, Devoxx, O’Reilly Fluent, NFJS SpringOne, AnDevCon, JAX, Open Web Camp, Silicon Valley Code Camp, HTML5DevConf, On Android and AjaxWorld. Kevin is the co-author of Web 2.0 Fundamentals. In the past Kevin was an adjunct professor at the College of San Mateo. Kevin holds a MS and BS in Computer Science from Southern Illinois University. Kevin is the leader of the Silicon Valley Java User Group, Silicon Valley Google Developer Group, Silicon Valley JavaScript Meetup and Devoxx4Kids Bay Area.
