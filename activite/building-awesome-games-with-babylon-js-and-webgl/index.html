Building Awesome Games with Babylon.js & WebGL

Ven 20 13h 00 Salle Savoir-Faire Linux · Développement

HTML5 and JavaScript are everywhere! They are changing how we develop services, applications and now games. With the advent of cross-browser support for WebGL, we are starting to see games being developed with JavaScript, running on every platform and every device. Finally, you can develop your game with JavaScript with shaders, physics and audio through WebAudio.

In this session, I will show you how to create an awesome web game and mobile experience using Babylon.js & WebGL. I will also show you how to deploy your game in a few simple steps and share it with the world!

Rami Sayar

Rami Sayar is a technologist passionate about the intersection of art, design and the web. For the past 5 years, he has been experimenting with the bleeding edge of emerging technologies, determined to find use for them in everyday life. In his current role as a Developer Evangelist for Microsoft Canada in Montreal, Rami covers technical areas such as open source technologies, open data, and cloud computing, as well as growth hacking and marketing. He is a frequent international speaker and has spoken at prestigious conferences such as FITC, CUSEC, KWS and TEDx at HEC Montreal. Rami is prevented from sleeping by his curiosity for experimentation, his insistence on shipping high quality code and helping startups survive the grind.
